Beginners Journey: Greg Starts His Blog

Whats the worst that can happen? July 2024

Buckle up everyone, because this dad is diving headfirst into the unknown! While I spent years ensuring smooth, secure, and highly available communication threads in the Military I transitioned into troubleshooting tech issues for Fortune 300+ companies in the "Civilian World" where I really got my hands dirty. With hard work and lots of training, I progressed in my career wearing many different hats to include Global NoC Lead, Security Analyst, Enterprise Infrastructor Leader in many differnet roles, and Advisor. The real thrill for me has always been learning new things. So, here I am, launching a blog for the first time. It might be a little scary, but hey, that's the beauty of stepping outside your comfort zone. Heres to the chance to grow, explore, and maybe even inspire others to embrace the amazing world of STEM! Wish me luck but better yet, give me feedback! You can contact me with the contact link in the navigation bar on the top right.

About Me

I served in the U.S. Army, specializing in Network Switching Systems and was attached to a Patriot Missile System Battalion. After my deployment and Honorable discharge, I went to college in Jacksonville, FL for Computer Science. I have two beautiful and very intelligent daughters. I have more than 20 years professional IT experience. This page is made to learn and have fun. If its messed up, let me know. Im still learning :)
